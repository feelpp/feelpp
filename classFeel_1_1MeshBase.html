◆ renumber()

template<typename IndexT = uint32_type>
virtual void Feel::MeshBase< IndexT >::renumber ( )
protectedpure virtual

After loading/defining a mesh, we want to have as much locality as possible (elements/faces/nodes to be contiguous).

In order to do that the mesh elements/faces/nodes are renumbered. That will be then most helpful when generating the Dof table. This procedure should work also with comm().size() == 1

Implemented in Feel::Mesh0D< Shape, T, IndexT >, Feel::Mesh1D< Shape, T, IndexT >, Feel::Mesh2D< Shape, T, IndexT >, and Feel::Mesh3D< Shape, T, IndexT >.
